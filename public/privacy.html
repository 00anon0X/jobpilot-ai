Privacy

Privacy policy for the JobPilot AI beta

Plain-English beta policy. This is not legal advice; it is the operating policy for this test deployment.

What is stored

We store your beta account email, candidate profile text, pasted job text, generated deterministic analysis, application tracker state, and waitlist details if submitted.

What is not stored

No resume files, no payment card details, no LLM prompt logs, and no auto-apply credentials are collected by this beta.

Controls

The app supports account-scoped access, downloadable account export, and workspace deletion from the live app. Server-side data is stored on this deployment and protected with file permissions and basic rate limiting.

Do not paste

Do not paste national IDs, home addresses, references' private contact details, financial credentials, or employer-confidential material.

Contact

Use the beta waitlist form for pilot contact and deletion/support requests if browser deletion is unavailable.
